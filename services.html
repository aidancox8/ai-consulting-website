Good to Know

Before you reach out

Free discovery call, always

Every engagement starts with a free 30-minute call. No commitment, no sales pressure — just an honest look at your situation and whether we're a good fit.

Fixed-price quotes

You'll receive a written scope and a fixed price before any work begins. No surprises, no hourly billing that runs over.

Pricing is flexible

Package prices reflect typical scope. Simpler projects come in at the lower end; larger or more complex builds at the higher end. We'll scope it accurately.

You don't need to replace your tools

Everything we build works within what you already pay for — primarily Microsoft 365. No new software subscriptions required in most cases.
